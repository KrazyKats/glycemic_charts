Not All Meals Are Created Equal

Some meals spike your blood sugar by a lot. Other meals, not so much. It varies from person to person. And it varies based on a bunch of factors. One particular (and perhaps intuitive) factor that influences glucose response to a meal is its macronutrient content.

Let's explore that!
